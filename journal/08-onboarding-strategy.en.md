## Background

First day at Hyundai Capital. Internal-only framework. No documentation. No onboarding process.

## Analysis

I went directly to people on other teams using the same framework. The key was how I asked.

![Question strategy comparison](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/juhyeonl-hub/portfolio-blog/main/journal/images/08_questions.png)

Form hypotheses from prior investigation, then ask for confirmation.

![Onboarding strategy flow](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/juhyeonl-hub/portfolio-blog/main/journal/images/08_onboarding.png)

Fully productive within a month. The project lead invited me to continue on the next project.

## Reflection

Fast adaptation isn't talent — it's strategy. "Admitting what you don't know" and "asking without trying anything first" are different. The former builds trust. The latter erodes it.

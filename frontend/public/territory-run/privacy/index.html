Territory Run — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-16 · Terms of Service

Who we are

Territory Run is operated by an individual developer based in Finland. Contact: xx.juon@gmail.com. We are the data controller for the data described below.

What we collect

  • Location (precise GPS) — used solely to compute which map tiles you claim and to verify sessions. Recording runs only while tracking is on (a persistent notification is always shown while recording; you can pause anytime).
  • Step count — used solely to verify that movement is on foot.
  • Account — an anonymous account id created automatically on first launch. No name, email, or phone number is required to play.
  • Body weight/height (optional) — stored on your device only, used to estimate calories. Never transmitted.

How it is stored

Game data is stored on our server (Supabase, EU region — Stockholm): your anonymous account id, chosen battles and teams, claimed tiles, and per-session summaries (distance, duration, verification result).

Raw GPS points of a session are deleted immediately after verification. Exceptions, kept strictly for fair-play review:

  • Sessions put on hold by verification keep their raw points for 7 days — so they can be re-judged in your favor if you appeal or the rules improve — then they are deleted.
  • A coarse route summary (30-second resolution grid cells) is kept for 14 days solely to detect multi-account abuse, then deleted.
  • Expired data is deleted automatically every day.

User-created battles

Battle names, team names and colors you create are visible to the people you invite (battles are private by default). Reports you file are stored with your account id for moderation purposes only.

What we never do

  • We do not sell your data.
  • We do not share your data with third parties, except when required by law (see our Terms of Service).
  • We do not use your location for advertising. The app has no ads.

Your rights (GDPR)

You can access, correct, or erase your data at any time: in-app (Profile → Reset data) or by contacting us. Erasure requests are honored within 30 days. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Finnish Data Protection Ombudsman (tietosuoja.fi).

Children

Territory Run is not directed at children under 13.

Changes

Material changes to this policy will be announced in-app.

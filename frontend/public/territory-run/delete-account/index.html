Territory Run — Delete Your Account

Last updated: 2026-07-17 · Privacy Policy

Delete in the app (instant)

  1. Open Territory Run
  2. Go to Profile (bottom tab)
  3. Scroll down and tap Delete account
  4. Confirm twice — deletion is immediate and cannot be undone

What gets deleted

  • Your account and anonymous identifier
  • All session records, points, badges, and reports you filed
  • Any remaining location data (raw traces, route summaries)
  • Tiles you hold are returned to their previous owners or become neutral

Battle names you created remain in the game (with the creator anonymized), so ongoing seasons are not disrupted for other players.
